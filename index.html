About

I am a Frontend Web Developer and most of my time I do Figma or PSD to html/css conversions. I build div-based layouts that are interactive, standards-compliant XHTML/HTML5/CSS which are always hand coded and Responsive. If you need a portfolio for your work, a presentation page, or a landing page that can attract customers, you are in the right place!


My projects are well-structured, a code is clean, semantic and easy-to-maintain. I am familiar with the latest front-end development trends and best practices, and I constantly strive to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field.

Here are my main advantages:

✔️ clean, flexible, semantic code;

✔️ should be responsive for any screen width up to 320px, for every pixel;

✔️ compliance with the design should be close to Pixel Perfect. I check using app;

✔️ no content duplication. Those. for mobile and desktop, the content should be the same, only changing with the help of media queries in CSS;

✔️ should look the same on all browsers and operating systems;

✔️ if I use Tailwind or Bootstrap - maximum use of the capabilities of the framework with its functionality and classes.

My goal №1

My №1 goal will always be to meet your needs and deadline. When working on a new project, I like to chat with the client, so that I can have a clear understanding of his needs and vision of the project.
